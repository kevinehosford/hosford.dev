### Overview
Pulled the big nexus sync into main, picking up React 19.2, TanStack Start/Router bumps, AI SDK v5, and zod v4. Locked down the EU build path with region-aware middleware so Vercel can't quietly schedule us in the wrong place, and patched the crash-of-the-week list along the way.

### Highlights
- [#5847](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5847) — Synced with `nexus`: bumped React to 19.2, refreshed TanStack Start/Router, AI SDK v5, zod v4, swapped `query-string-for-all` for `query-string` with our own `stringifySearch`/`parseSearch` hooks, switched root SSR to data-only, and rebuilt the OIDC IDP-initiated callback at `/frapi/auth/oauth2/callback/axiom` with a real error page. Telemetry init moved into the root (Sentry, Segment, FontAwesome) and Spotlight finally ditched TRPC for `POST /frapi/spotlight/summary` plus a feedback endpoint.
- [#5892](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5892), [axiomhq/axiom#14967](https://github.com/axiomhq/axiom/pull/14967) — New `vercelRegionMiddleware` enforces the EU allow-list before tracing/logging boot, and `vercelResourceAttributes` now rides along in every log/trace context. Paired with letting us authenticate against `*.axiom.dev` so EU staged deploys are actually checkable before they hit `app.eu`.
- [#5851](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5851), [#5885](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5885) — Bar chart and log scale stopped crashing once `aggChartOpts` and `overlayCharts` got typed properly and parsed with a fallback instead of throwing; `clearForm` now strips both from the URL. Dashboard annotations were similarly unfixable by clicking — rewrapped them in the new `MenuProvider`/`MenuButton`/`Menu` primitives and deleted the deprecated `AnnotationsDropdown`.
- [#5859](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5859), [#5868](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5868), [#5883](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/5883) — Routine maintenance: `@axiomhq/monaco-apl` 0.0.18, Playwright 1.56.0 across the CI container and dev deps, and dropped `E2E Tests` from the required check list so the rest of CI can gate merges while we keep stabilizing the suite.
