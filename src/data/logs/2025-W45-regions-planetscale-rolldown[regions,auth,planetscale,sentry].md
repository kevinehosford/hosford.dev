### Overview
Two big themes: finished the region-selection UX for new orgs, and centralized session/token/org resolution into a single middleware stack so we stopped hammering Planetscale on every request. Took a swing at switching the build over to rolldown-vite, then reverted it the same week. Also sorted the Sentry release pipeline so prod and prod-eu actually upload sourcemaps.

### Highlights
- [#6028](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6028) — Let people pick a default region during org creation with EU-specific copy in the modal, gated the new picker behind a feature flag, and surfaced the computed region in org settings so it's obvious where the data lives once you click through.
- [#6031](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6031) — Pulled session, access-token, and org resolution out of every individual loader/handler into global `getSessionContext` / `getAccessTokenContext` / `getOrgContext` middleware, plus `ensureAuthorization` / `ensureAccessToken` / `ensureOrgId` enforcers. Cut the Planetscale session-token query count way down and tightened CSRF/auth in the process.
- [#6051](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6051), [#6071](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6071) — Tried switching the frontend to rolldown-vite with a custom `kusto-bridge-fix` rollup plugin and a runtime `Bridge.init()` call to make the Kusto language service load in the right order. Reverted back to Vite 7 a couple of days later — wasn't ready for us yet.
- [#6046](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6046), [#6048](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6048), [#6043](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6043) — Added `LEGACY_APP_INGEST_API_URL` for the Vercel ingest proxy and switched logs/traces to read from `AXIOM_URL` with `/v1` paths; then split traces back out to `AXIOM_TRACING_URL` so the OTel collector can have its own endpoint. Also stopped the `/_authed` SSR loader from prefetching `userStatusQuery` and `getUserSettingsQuery` since the un-awaited prefetch was breaking hydration.
- [#6074](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6074), [#6078](https://github.com/axiomhq/app/pull/6078) — Sentry release version is now computed as `1.2.3-<commithash>` in production (the lag between merges and deploys means the package version alone isn't accurate), with the SHA threaded through from CI as `PUBLIC_COMMITHASH`. Bumped `@sentry/vite-plugin` to 4.3.0 and passed `SENTRY_ENVIRONMENT`/`SENTRY_AUTH_TOKEN` to both prod and prod-eu builds so the uploads actually happen.
